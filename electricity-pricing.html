    Upon receipt of payment, the customer's order is submitted to ERCOT; the account will typically go "active" within 3 to 5 business days for standard service and same day or next day for priority service. The customer will then be texted and or e-mailed daily on their usage and balance. Along with the daily usage, the customer will also get an estimation on the number of days that service will last. If the balance falls below $0.00 a disconnect order will be sent out. Once the minimum balance is met a reconnect order is sent out and the electricity is normally reconnected within 2 hours.
